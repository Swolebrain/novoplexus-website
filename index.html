Blockchain-powered

NovoPlexus uses a secure, de-centralized network, which encrypts and remembers the location of each part of your medical record without vulnerable servers. Now, just like your financial records, you’ll have total access to your medical records through your unique NovoPlexus blockchain ID, even if the records are in many different places. Doctors and other healthcare providers can also enjoy access to the network without changing their current office systems.

AI-driven

Social media and shopping networks use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to help learn more about their customers and so does NovoPlexus. AI can help pick up patterns in your health, like when you typically see the doctor most often, to help make useful suggestions about diet, nutrition or preventative care. AI can also help your doctor quickly find the most important parts of your records to help you feel better faster.

Healthcare Marketplace

Beyond consolidating your medical records, the NovoPlexus network allows you to easily and safely interact with the entire medical community in real-time. Imaging having doctors, hospitals, pharmacies, insurance companies and a vast array of health and wellness professionals all at your fingertips! This dramatically increases efficiency, saving time and money for everyone, even if you don’t have insurance. And by paying the same, low access charge, everyone is treated equally on the network.

About the Founders

William Reisacher, MD

Co-Founder and Senior Medical Director

Dr. Reisacher is an Associate Professor of Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery and Director of Allergy Services at Weill Cornell Medical College/NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital in NYC. He graduated from Cornell University in 1990 and graduated with Distinction in Research from The Mount Sinai School of Medicine in 1994. Dr. Reisacher is a Fellow of the American Academy of Otolaryngic Allergy (AAOA), where he has also served on the Board of Directors. He has been a co-founder, inventor and senior advisor for several early-stage companies in the biotechnology and digital health-tech spaces.

Prakash Hosalli

Co-Founder and CEO

Prakash Hosalli is an Entrepreneurial leader focused on bringing cutting edge technologies including Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Big Data to solve vexing problems in Healthcare. He’s a graduate of National Defence Academy, Pune India and Indian Military Academy with 8 years of combat experience in the Indian Army as a Paratrooper/Infantry officer. He has more than 18 years of Technology and Business experience as Program, Project, and Product manager running large multi-national teams, handling Sales, P&L and Business relationship with marquee clients across US.
